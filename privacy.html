Leone Tours

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Leone Tours ("the app", "we", "us") handles information when you use the Leone Tours Android application. We are committed to transparency and to protecting the privacy of all users, including the aged care residents and staff who use this app.

1. Who We Are

Leone Tours is an independent Android application developed to provide GPS-triggered audio tours and entertainment for aged care residents, primarily operated on bus trips managed by care facility staff.

2. Data We Collect

Leone Tours collects minimal data. The only category of user data that leaves the device is approximate location, used solely to retrieve current weather conditions.

  • Approximate Location: When the weather feature is active, your device's approximate GPS coordinates are sent to Open-Meteo (a free, open-source weather API) to retrieve local weather information. This is used to personalise audio commentary within the app. Open-Meteo does not store or share your location data — it is processed ephemerally to service the request in real time.

All other app data — including tour settings, playlists, location spot configurations, and play history — is stored locally on your device only in a SQLite database and never transmitted to us or any third party.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

Leone Tours does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • Financial or payment information
  • Photos, videos, or audio recordings from users
  • Contacts or calendar data
  • Usage analytics or crash reporting
  • Advertising identifiers

4. Third-Party Services

The app interacts with the following third-party services:

  • Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com) — Receives approximate device location to return weather data. No account or API key required. See their terms.
  • Cloudflare R2 — Used to stream audio content (music and tour audio) to the app. No personal user data is transmitted; requests are standard HTTPS file downloads.

5. Permissions Used

  • Location (foreground & background): Used to detect proximity to GPS tour spots and to retrieve weather data. Location is never stored remotely.
  • Storage: Used to read local audio files and save app data on-device.
  • Notifications: Used for Android Auto media controls and background playback indicators.

6. Data Retention

No personal data is retained by Leone Tours on any server. All data stored by the app exists locally on the user's device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app or using the in-app backup/restore tools.

7. Children's Privacy

Leone Tours is designed for use by adults, primarily aged care residents and facility staff. The app is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date below. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
